# Kansei-App-Music-Beta-

This music application is designed to provide a flexible and personalized listening experience, similar to Spotify. Users can not only stream music but also modify their own content by adding custom songs to the platform. The app allows users to create and manage their own music library, organize playlists, and tailor their listening experience based on their preferences.

With a user-centered design, this application gives full control to users over their music collection, making it more than just a streaming service‚ÄĒit becomes a customizable music ecosystem.
